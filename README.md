# WATERING MAN









Watering Man is a boss rush / shooter game. The controls are run, jump, and shoot. In this game, flower demons are breaking into your house and you must vanquish them. Shoot all the flower demons with water drops to win!

### FEATURES

- Run and Jump to avoid taking damage from the Flower demons. Player starts with three lives. If a player is touched by a flower demon they will lose a life and have 2 seconds of invincibility to get away. 
<br>
<img width="330" alt="Screen Shot 2021-12-16 at 11 46 05 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/88504868/146413896-3002390c-abf3-4deb-9265-a8f60d57c9d7.png">

- Shoot water droplets at the flower demons to damage them. They will pause for a moment when they are hit. Use this time to make it to a safer spot in the house! The demons do not have a health bar, so keep shooting until they disappear. <br>
